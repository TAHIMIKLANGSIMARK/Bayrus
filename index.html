Few information about cars

Toyota is one of the largest and most well-known car manufacturers in the world, known for producing reliable, fuel-efficient, and high-quality vehicles. Here are some key points about Toyota: 1. Company Overview: Founded: 1937 in Japan by Kiichiro Toyoda Headquarters: Toyota City, Japan Global Presence: One of the top car brands worldwide 2. Popular Toyota Models: Sedans: Toyota Camry, Toyota Corolla SUVs: Toyota RAV4, Toyota Highlander, Toyota Land Cruiser Trucks: Toyota Tacoma, Toyota Tundra Hybrids/Electric: Toyota Prius, Toyota Mirai (Hydrogen Fuel Cell), Toyota bZ4X (EV) 3. Notable Features: Reliability: Toyota cars are known for their durability and low maintenance costs. Hybrid Leadership: The Toyota Prius was one of the first mass-produced hybrid cars. Safety: Many models include Toyota Safety Sense (TSS), which provides advanced driver-assist features. Off-Road Capability: The Toyota Land Cruiser and 4Runner are well-known for their off-road performance. 4. Toyota's Innovations: Hybrid Technology: Pioneered hybrid cars with the Prius. Hydrogen Fuel Cell Vehicles: The Toyota Mirai is one of the first hydrogen-powered consumer cars. Electric Vehicles (EVs): Toyota is expanding its EV lineup with models like the bZ4X. 5. Fun Fact: Toyota's best-selling car of all time is the Toyota Corolla, with over 50 million units sold worldwide!
